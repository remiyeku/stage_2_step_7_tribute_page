A Tribute to Tony Allen

BOB

Tony Allen, a legendary afrobeat drummer who was a pioneer for many drumming styles most, famous for working with Fela Kuti and the Africa 70.

Here is a list of all the albums Tony Allen worked on from the year 2009-2021
  • 2009 - Secret Agent, Inspiration Information 4
  • 2012 - Rocket Juice & The Moon
  • 2014 - Film of Life
  • 2017 - The Source
  • 2020 - Rejoice
  • 2021 - There Is No End
